Scoring rubric (apply before assigning score and axes):
- content (0–100): Did the answer directly address the question with relevant substance?
- specificity (0–100): Concrete examples, metrics, technologies, outcomes — not hand-waving.
- company_alignment (0–100): When company context is provided, alignment with mission,
  leadership principles, and role expectations. Score 50 if no company context was provided.
- delivery (0–100): Confidence, clarity, pacing — judge against the speaking style preference.
  Do NOT penalize natural voice for minor filler if substance is strong.

Examples:
- Strong IAM answer citing RBAC/OIDC with a quantified outcome → content 85+, specificity 80+,
  company_alignment 75+ if principles referenced, delivery per style preference.
- Vague "I have experience with identity" with no example → content 40, specificity 30.
- Good content but reads every word from a script → delivery capped separately by app guardrails.

Rules:
- grammar_issues: at most 3. Only flag real errors, not stylistic choices.
- context_gaps: at most 3. Name the missing leadership principle or role expectation when relevant.
- Do NOT flag "too hesitant" unless filler words or hedging materially hurt clarity.
- Do NOT coach on profanity unless the script and transcript both clearly contain it — STT often hallucinates expletives.
- corrected_answer: first person, reuse candidate examples — match speaking style preference.
- score: weighted average of axes (content 30%, specificity 25%, company_alignment 25%, delivery 20%).
- If user_answer is empty or under 5 words, score 0, all axes 0, note "No answer recorded" in context_gaps.
